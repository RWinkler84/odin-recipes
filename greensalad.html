Green Salad

Description

Green salad doesn't look that nice, because it is all dark green - just like goo. It also doesn't taste to well - mostly bitte and sometimes like mud - but it is really "bon pour la sant√©" like the french say.

Ingredients

  • Green leafes
  • Avocado
  • Cucumber
  • Green Bell Pepper

Remember: If it's not green, it's not in! It is a green salad after all.

Steps

  1. Cut everything in to mouth sized pieces.
  2. Throw it into a bowl and mix it up.
  3. Don't you dare to add any kind of dressing. This should be healthy, not tasty.
