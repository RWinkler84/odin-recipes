Lasagna

Description

This lasagna is without doubt the best lasagna you will ever eat. It is creamy, chewy and savory. It has everything you want. Including a ton of calories.

Serve this to your friends, family or enemies. They will love you and later die of cardiac arrest, because of their way to fatty diet. What could be better?

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Tomato paste
  • Tons of cheese
  • Whipped Cream
  • Ground pork
  • Lots of salt
  • Herbs

Steps

Come on your not stupid. You know how to make a tasty lasagna! No? Really? Okay, here we go!

  1. Get a baking form deep enough to hold your lasagna.
  2. Mix the wet ingredients together and stirr well.
  3. Add all the spices. Stirr again until everything is well combined.
  4. Add the ground porc in small digestable chunks to your mixture.
  5. Layer your lasagna starting with your meat sauce, followed by a cover of pasta sheets.
  6. Repeat until your container is almost full.
  7. Top everything off with a thick layer of ground cheese. Don't be thrifty on that one. This adds a lot of taste, but also fat and salt to your dish and will make your guests want an extra serving. Remember: It's not murder, if they beg for it. ;)
